Who We Are

Alibaba Group is a global leader in e-commerce, retail, internet, and technology. Founded in 1999 by Jack Ma and a team of 17 people in Hangzhou, China, Alibaba has grown into one of the world's largest online commerce companies.

What We Do

We provide platforms for small businesses to reach global customers through our various services like Alibaba.com, Taobao, and Tmall. Our mission is to make it easy to do business anywhere.

Our Vision

To build the future infrastructure of commerce, serving millions of buyers and sellers around the world with smart, efficient, and secure online services.
